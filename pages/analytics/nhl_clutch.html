NHL Clutchness - Goaltenders

Clutchness = 0.7 * Absolute Clutchness + 0.3 * Elevation in Clutchtime

Absolute Clutchness = Goalie performance in Clutchtime

Clutchtime = Any combination of the playoffs, when the score is within 1, or when the goalie's team is on the penalty kill

*To qualify, a goalie must have at a minimum of 5 minutes of Clutch TOI

Data provided via Natural Stat Trick
