NHL Player Clutchness

This model is only displaying goalie stats right now, skater clutch stats will be introduced later.

Clutchtime can be defined by any combination of scenarios that involve the playoffs, the penalty kill, or the score being within 1. The Clutchness rating is calculated by combining a goalie's save percentage, high danger save percentage, and GSAx/60 in those scenarios, then factoring in how much their game is elevated in clutchtime as well.

To qualify, a goalie must have at a minimum of 60 minutes of Clutch TOI.

Data provided via Natural Stat Trick
