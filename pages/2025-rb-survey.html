Kimmetrics 2025 NFL Draft Position Group Survey

It's time for the 2nd annual Kimmetrics NFL Draft position group survey!

Originally, I wanted to do another addition of my quarterback survey from last year, but the quarterback class this year is much less intriguing than last year's stacked QB class. Therefore, I decided to choose a different positional group for this draft class, and the answer was obvious. I decided to go with the surprisingly stacked running back class this year.

This will be interesting, because running backs are more situation-dependent than quarterbacks, and it is much more unpredictable which teams will draft a running back and in what round. To address this, I am focusing this year's survey more on the talent of the players rather than asking people to predict their career trajectory.

You can take the survey here and be sure to check back for the results after the draft!
